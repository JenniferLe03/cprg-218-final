Hi, I'm Jennifer Le

A User Experience (UX) Designer who believes that great design creates meaningful experiences, fosters connections, and brings ease to everyday interactions with products. I focus on understanding people, crafting intuitive, user-friendly designs that make life simpler and more enjoyable for all.

About Me

My journey into UX/UI design began at a small startup, where I transitioned from client experience to a leadership role. There, I gained hands-on experience conducting user research, designing workflows and helping develop an MVP product, all while fostering a collaborative team environment. Since then, I enrolled at SAIT, where I refined my UX/UI skills and expanded my knowledge in web development, product management, and graphic design.

With a background in Social Work and counseling, I bring a user-focused mindset to every project. My curiosity about human behaviour drives me to design functional, thoughful solutions that make a meaningful impact on our community.

Outside of design, I love exploring my creative side through illustration, crafting and diving deeper into studying design theory and fundamentals.
